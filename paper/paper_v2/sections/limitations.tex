\section{Limitations}

LLM runs are slower and can be sensitive to local runtime load.
Confidence intervals are finite-sample and remain wider on easy tier.
Hard\_v1 shows saturation in smoke profile and is not used as primary mechanism-comparison evidence; once collapse saturates at 1.0 and survival at 0.0 across conditions, governance effects become non-identifiable under floor effects.
The current substrate is intentionally minimal, so external validity to richer socio-ecological systems depends on future re-tests with added heterogeneity, shocks, and institutional complexity.
